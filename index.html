Discover Seoul

A City of Tradition, Innovation, and Infinite Discoveries

Welcome to Seoul

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a bustling metropolis where ancient traditions meet cutting-edge technology. As one of Asia’s most vibrant cities, it offers visitors a unique mix of historical sites and futuristic urban landscapes.

Location & Geography

Seoul is located in the northwest part of South Korea, bordered by mountains and the Han River. It is known for its scenic beauty and its role as the country's cultural and economic center.

Historical Significance

With a history that spans more than 2,000 years, Seoul has served as the capital of various Korean dynasties, including the Joseon Dynasty. The city's palaces, temples, and traditional villages stand as reminders of its rich past.

Why Visit Seoul?

Whether you’re exploring centuries-old palaces, indulging in Korean street food, or shopping in its bustling markets, Seoul offers an endless array of experiences for every traveler. It’s a city where the past and the future come together to create a truly unique travel destination.

Top Attractions

Bukchon Hanok Village

Walk through centuries of history with views of the beautiful hanok houses and narrow alleyways.

Dongdaemun Design Plaza

A cultural hub and architectural marvel designed by Zaha Hadid, offering museums, exhibitions, and public spaces.

Lotte World Tower

The tallest skyscraper in Korea, featuring an observation deck with stunning views of Seoul.
